 Version Notation 
 IFC versions are identified using he notation " Major.Minor.Addendum.Corrigendum ".


 Major versions consis of scope expansions or deletions and may have changes ha break compatibility.


 Minor versions consis of feature extensions, where compatibility is guaranteed for he "core" schema, bu no for other definitions.


 Addendums consis of improvements o existing features, where he schema may change bu upward compatibility is guaranteed.


 Corrigendums consis of improvements o documentation, where he schema does no change hough deprecation is possible.
